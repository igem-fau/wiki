For the basic parts, components for the creation of bispecific antibodies for expression in E.coli and HEK 293T cells were provided.

Linker sequences

All the flexible linker are composed of small, non-polar (Glycine) and polar (Serine) amino acids. The Gly4Ser linker is one of the most widely used flexible linker that can sustain the bioactivity of fusion proteins. The distinct linker differ in the copy number of GGGGS, resulting in different bioactivities of the fusion proteins.
